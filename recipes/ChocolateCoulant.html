Chocolate Coulant

Chocolate volcano.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 100 gr. sugar
  • 40 gr. Of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 200 gr. chocolate for desserts
  • 80 gr. of butter

Steps

  1. To prepare the chocolate coulant, we will start by putting the oven to heat to 180ºC, heat up and down.
  2. In a bowl we put the butter and chocolate, in another the eggs and sugar.
  3. Put the chocolate and butter in the microwave or we can melt it in a bain-marie, on the other hand we beat the sugar and eggs with the rods.
  4. Add the sifted flour and mix little by little.
  5. In this mixture we will put the chocolate that we have melted together with the butter and cocoa powder.
  6. Mix with a spatula with enveloping movements, little by little until all the chocolate is well mixed.
  7. Prepare some molds and spread them with a little butter.
  8. Fill the molds filling them ¾ parts of their capacity, as they rise.
  9. Put the molds in the oven for 7 minutes, look at the upper part of the coulant, when you see them without the chocolate shine they are ready.
  10. Remove them from the oven, we remove them from the molds very carefully and serve hot.
