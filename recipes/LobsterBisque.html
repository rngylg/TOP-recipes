Lobster Bisque

For rich, creamy lobster bisque, start with a deeply flavorful lobster-shell stock.

Ingredients

  • 560g (4) live lobsters
  • 115g unsalted butter
  • 80ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 340g medium carrots
  • 450g medium yellow onions
  • 170g large celery ribs
  • 4 medium garlic cloves
  • 25g tomato paste
  • 60ml brandy
  • 240ml dry white wine
  • 1.2L chicken stock
  • 3 sprigs parsley
  • 3 sprigs tarragon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 120ml heavy cream
  • salt and pepper
  • cayenne pepper
  • fresh chives
  • grounded coriander seeds

Steps

  1. kill and split lobsters
  2. Steam lobsters
  3. Remove meat from shell
