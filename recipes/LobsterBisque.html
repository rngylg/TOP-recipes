Lobster Bisque

Rich & creamy lobster bisque

Ingredients

  • 560g (4) live lobsters
  • 115g unsalted butter
  • 80ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 340g medium carrots
  • 450g medium yellow onions
  • 170g large celery ribs
  • 4 medium garlic cloves
  • 25g tomato paste
  • 60ml brandy
  • 240ml dry white wine
  • 1.2L chicken stock
  • 3 sprigs parsley
  • 3 sprigs tarragon
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 120ml heavy cream
  • salt and pepper
  • cayenne pepper
  • fresh chives
  • grounded coriander seeds

Steps

  1. Kill and split lobsters
  2. Steam lobsters for 3 mins
  3. Remove meat from shell
  4. Chop up bodies and shells
  5. Brown the bodies and shells with butter and olive oil
  6. Add carrot, celery, onion, continue to cook and brown
  7. Add tomato paste, brandy, white wine, lobster juices & chicken stock
  8. Strain, then blend the pot
  9. Add lobster meat and serve
