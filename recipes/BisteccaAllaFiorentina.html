Bistecca alla Fiorentina

The king of all steaks, Bistecca Alla Fiorentina.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 pound bone-in porterhouse steak
  • Fresh rosemary, sage, thyme
  • Salt & pepper, garlic & onion
  • Olive oil and unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Make the baster by wrapping the fresh herbs up in butcher's twine.
  2. Next, brush both sides of the steak with olive oil.
  3. Season the steak on both sides with salt, pepper, onion, and garlic granules.
  4. Cook it for 4-6 minutes per side for a beautiful rare steak.
  5. Baste it with the butter and brush it with the fresh herbs
  6. Brushing it one last time with the butter and herbs and carving the cuts of steak away from the bone.
  7. Slice the strip steak and filet mignon away from the T-bone and then slice those cuts on a bias and serve it all next to the bone for a cool rustic look.
