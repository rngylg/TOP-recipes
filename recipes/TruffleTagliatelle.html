Truffle Tagliatelle

This aromatic sauce is the perfect match to the long silken strands of tagliatelle, made by our pasta artisans using simple, high-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 pound tagliatelle
  • 1 whole black truffle
  • 0.5 tablespoon Urbani truffle butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring 3 quarts of water to a boil
  2. Cook tagliatelle until tender
  3. Heat truffle butter until melted
  4. Add garlic, salt & pepper, and grate in the black truffle
  5. Add pasta to pan, mix with sauce
